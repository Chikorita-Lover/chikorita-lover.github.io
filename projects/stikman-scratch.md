The first _Stikman_ game released, _Stikman Scratch_ is a short side-scrolling platforming game made in Scratch. Play as Stikman as he chases down the Devil Dancers and retrieves his Golden Sticks!

Play through six short levels, defeating enemies and using items to reach the goal. Test your skills – and your blade – against the Devil Dancers and the enemies that live on Mount Madeline.

Initially released in March 2018, _Stikman Scratch_ would receive updates up to and through 2020. While the game itself is short and simple, it has defined the main characters and principles of the _Stikman_ series today.
