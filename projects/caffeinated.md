Caffeinated is the first _Minecraft: Java Edition_ mod released by Chikorita Lover. The mod is an expansion upon Chikorita Lover's Coffee Add-On, an add-on for _Minecraft_ released over a year prior. Caffeinated features coffee plants, which can be harvested and brewed through a unique brewing method!

Coffee plants are found in jungle biomes, and can be harvested to attain coffee berries. Coffee berries can be made into coffee beans, which can then be planted or crafted further into ground coffee. Using a cauldron, some water, a heat source, and ground coffee, players can brew a pot coffee and bottle it up for consumption. Coffee can be drunk to provide the player with energy and preserve their food points, or crafted into tiramisu for an even stronger boost.

Caffeinated is available on both Modrinth and CurseForge for the Fabric modloader. Caffeinated features compatibility with Farmer's Delight.
