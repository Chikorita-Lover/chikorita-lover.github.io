Kaleidoscope is a large quality-of-life mod released for _Minecraft: Java Edition_. The mod features many new building blocks, items, and changes that improve the base game in small yet meaningful ways. Kaleidoscope is carefully designed to be a true "kaleidoscope" of colorful features. Players who are familiar with Quark or Supplementaries may especially find the mod enjoyable!

Kaleidoscope does not greatly affect the main game loop of _Minecraft_, but focuses instead on expanding smaller portions of the game. Craft items and blocks with new workstations and recipes, touch up your builds with new decorative blocks, and explore new ways to interact with the entities and world around you. The [Kaleidoscope Wiki](https://github.com/Chikorita-Lover/Kaleidoscope/wiki) lists each and every item and feature introduced in the mod.

Kaleidoscope is available on both Modrinth and CurseForge for the Fabric modloader.
