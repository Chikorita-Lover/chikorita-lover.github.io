The last _Stikman_ game released on Scratch, _Stikman's Legend_ is a top-down action-adventure game that tells an alternate story to that of _Stikman Scratch_. Explore the wilds of Mount Madeline as Stikman as he seeks the Golden Sticks to escape to Ormali City!

_Stikman's Legend_ features a small open-world environment featuring different items, enemies, and characters. Common enemies like Bootrap and Handoom return from _Stikman Scratch_, while new characters and weapons make their first appearance here. Different weapons and items each provide unique abilities that will aid you, the player, in guiding Stikman to freedom.

_Stikman's Legend_ was originally created as a short demo on September 21, 2019 as part of [University of Nebraska–Lincoln's CSE Hack Day](https://newsroom.unl.edu/announce/cse/10108/59704). The project was created not just by Chikorita Lover, but also Brody S., Gabriel L., and Malachi M. _Stikman's Legend_ was later updated to become a full game by Chikorita Lover alone.
