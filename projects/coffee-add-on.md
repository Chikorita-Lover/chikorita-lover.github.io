Chikorita Lover's Coffee Add-On is one of two add-ons for _Minecraft_ released by Chikorita Lover. The add-on adds coffee as crops found in jungle biomes that can be harvested and grown by players. Harvested coffee can then be crafted into decorative blocks, traded to villagers, or brewed into drinkable coffee!

Coffee berries are harvested from coffee plants, which are then crafted into coffee beans and then into ground coffee. Bottles of coffee can be brewed with ground coffee using a brewing stand, which prevent phantoms from targeting players when drunk.

Coffee Add-On is small in scope and no longer supported. However, a successor to Coffee Add-On – [Caffeinated](/projects/caffeinated) – was released for _Minecraft: Java Edition_ in June 2023, redesigning how coffee works and expanding upon the base content in general.
