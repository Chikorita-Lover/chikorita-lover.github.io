Bamboo Burnishes is the second of two add-ons released for _Minecraft_ by Chikorita Lover. It is a small yet simple add-on that adds three new decorative blocks crafted with bamboo: bamboo planks, bamboo pillars, and bamboo lanterns.

With the release of _Minecraft: Trails & Tales_, bamboo blocks have since been added to vanilla _Minecraft_. As a result, Bamboo Burnishes is no longer supported.
