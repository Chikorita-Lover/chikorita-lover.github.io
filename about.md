# About
Welcome to The Library of Roosey -- an evergrowing archive of all of my works, thoughts, and interests over the years. This web site is a passion project of mine and is something I work on as I please.

I started this site in late February 2026. The site is still under some construction, so there are likely to be a handful of changes over time!

The main purpose of this site is documenting any projects I did and do work on and my thoughts regarding my projects and others'. You can find all this information in the form of [pages for each project](/projects/) and posts over on the [blog](/blog).

## About the author
_Who's running this operation, anyways?_

Hello -- I'm **Chikorita Lover**, a programmer, a bit of an artist, and video game enthusiast. I also go by the names **Roosey** and **Calvin**, so don't be alarmed if you see me or others throw those around.

I'm a big fan of video games, particularly _Pokémon_, _Minecraft_, and various indie titles. (Yes, my favorite Pokémon is Chikorita -- but I love so many more, too!) Much of what I work on pertains to those interests or my own creations, specifically _Stikman_. I don't put out finished products often, but when I do, I can assure you I've put my entire being into them.

I got into programming as a child through Scratch back in 2016. I was immediately hooked, and now I love working on any programming, whether its video games or applications. I'm currently studying software engineering at university, and while I'm not seeking a job in designing games, it is something I would love to do in my own time.

Don't be a stranger! If you have any comments, concerns, or cuestions -- about the site, my projects, or otherwise -- feel free to [contact me](/contact).
