# About
Hello – I'm **Chikorita Lover**, a programmer and video game enthusiast. I also go by the names **Roosey** and **Calvin**, so don't be alarmed if you see me throw those around.

This is my website dedicated to highlighting my past and present work; I also share blog posts from time to time, sharing my thoughts on my and others' works. There's no set schedule, so if you are interested in what I share, please check back every so often!

I'm big into designing video games and mods and hacks for my favorite games. I don't put out finished products often but, when I do, I can assure you I've put my entire being into them. I'm currently studying software engineering in university, and while I'm not seeking a job in designing games, it is something I would love to do in my own time.

Don't be a stranger! If you have any questions or inquiries, feel free to reach out to me on [Twitter](https://x.com/ChikoritaL), [Tumblr](https://www.tumblr.com/chikorita-lover), or Discord (where my username is `chikorita_lover`).

Please keep in mind that this site is still under heavy construction.
